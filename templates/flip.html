Sketchy Flip Station

Quick play

Flip a Sketch is a simple heads-or-tails style dApp. Pick your side, choose the amount in the game window, confirm only the transaction you understand, then wait for the result.

Play Below Wallet Mode
Rules
  1. Connect only the wallet you want to play with.
  2. Pick the flip side and amount inside the game.
  3. Review gas, chain, contract, and spend amount before approving.
  4. The result is final after the transaction confirms.

Wallet safety

  • Never share your seed phrase or private key.
  • Do not approve a transaction that looks different from your play.
  • Use fullscreen wallet mode if your wallet blocks embedded dApps.
This dApp is embedded inside MyMilio with iframe restrictions. Wallets may open popups; confirm the URL and every wallet prompt before signing.
